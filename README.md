# Smart Tourism Intelligence Platform (STIP)

An AI-powered platform that uses tourism and traffic data to help governments and local businesses make smarter decisions through predictive analytics.

## Vision

To build a digital intelligence platform that enables sustainable tourism by forecasting crowd density, traffic, parking demand, emergency response needs, and business demand.

## Features

- Tourist crowd prediction
- Hourly density heatmaps
- Smart parking management
- Festival impact forecasting
- Carbon emissions estimation
- Emergency response planning
- Restaurant & café demand forecasting
- Inventory and food waste optimization
